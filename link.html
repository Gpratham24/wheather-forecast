About The Weather App

    Weather apps provide real-time weather information, forecasts, and other weather-related data. Some feature of weather apps include:

    Temperature:

    The high temperature for the day, and an hour-by-hour breakdown of temperatures

    Precipitation:

    Rainfall forecast

    Air quality:

    Humidity levels, UV level, and "feels like" temperature

    Other weather data:

    Time of sunrise and sunset, wind speed, and UV weather map
